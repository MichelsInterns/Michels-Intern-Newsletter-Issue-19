Company Spotlight
About Michels Pipeline
Michels Pipeline builds, fixes, and maintains pipelines across the country. Our team works on projects that safely move oil, gas, hydrogen, and water where they need to go. Crews also build and service pumping and compressor stations and handle emergency repairs when unexpected issues arise.
Project Highlight
Our team recently completed a 35‑MW PEM electrolyzer facility in Niagara Falls, NY, which uses water from the Niagara River to produce green hydrogen. This clean energy is then transported through new and existing infrastructure to support industries such as aerospace, electronics, and manufacturing.

We’re proud to help deliver one of the first green hydrogen facilities in the United States and to continue advancing the nation’s energy future.
